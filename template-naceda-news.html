Federal News

This is a headline

After one day of debate on the Senate's FY 2016 Transportation, Housing and Urban Development (THUD) appropriations bill, it was withdrawn on November 19 because of a controversial amendment relating to refugees that threatened to derail the passage of the bill. Congress must pass its appropriations bills or a new continuing resolution by December 11, when the current congressional resolution to fund all federal funded programs expires. Read more...

NACEDA News

Headline for NACEDA News

The amount of your dues may be different than in past years because the NACEDA membership voted to change the dues structure in 2015. Also new, you will have the opportunity to claim hardship or contribute a little extra with an additional assessment. If you have any questions, please let us know.

Want a reminder of what it means to be a NACEDA member? Take a look at "16 for 2016" on the new NACEDA website for a review of 2015 and a glimpse into 2016. The website is a work in progress. We welcome your suggestions.

We look forward to working together to build prosperity and maximize the power of place.
